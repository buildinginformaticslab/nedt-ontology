Coverage of the 15 competency questions (CQ1–CQ15) by existing ontologies vs. the CDT extension. CQ13–CQ15 were added in v1.2 (district-heating viability, group-heating candidates, LV-node flexibility). Each cell is scored s∈{0,1,2} (○ none / ◐ partial / ● full). The right-hand %-coverage column aggregates each row as Σs / (2 × nCQ) × 100, with nCQ=15 (max 30 score points per row). The Best-of existing (union) row takes the per-column maximum across all reused ontologies and shows the residual gap that the CDT extension closes. The CDT row (orange) reaches 100 % by construction. Matrix mirrors §9 of DT_ontology_paper.md.
